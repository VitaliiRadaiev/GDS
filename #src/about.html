The product of my passion to see young people succeed.

After working as a licence testing officer with VicRoads, it became my focus to work alongside young adults, guiding them on the path to becoming excellent solo drivers; an important life milestone and in many respects the start of one’s adult life.

Book a lesson

How did it happen?

I took the knowledge I gained from grading driving tests and applied my own skill in constructing learning systems to create a truly unique driver education program for my students.

Among other things, what sets GDS apart is our use of tech-based tools to aid in the learning process, making it more effective and engaging than otherwise would be the case. It gives me great joy when I see my students making substantial strides forward in their driving ability.

So...
what is
the process?

Book a lesson

The tasks we do during our lessons, and the theory components covered, will help shape the way you drive and assist you to make sound decisions on the road, even when faced with new, unexpected situations, with the end goal being to equip you with the right tools and attitude for a lifetime of safe driving.

My students approach me at different points in their driving journey. Some have never set foot in the driver’s seat of a car, others have done dozens of hours of practice already. Regardless of your current skill level, I aim to work constructively with you such that you get to a point where you’ll be satisfied in your own achievement and abilities, thus making the drive test itself a chance to showcase your skill, rather than something to dread.
