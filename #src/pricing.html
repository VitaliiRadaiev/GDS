NOTE!

  • With every IT booking, the expectation is that you (my student) will meet me at the VicRoads office, where your drive test is to be held, and make your own way from the office at the conclusion of the test.

  • If you require pick up from or drop off to a nearby railway station (or bus stop) in order to make it to or from the VicRoads office, this something that I am happy to accommodate, if notified beforehand.

  • GDS handles IT payments separately from lesson charges, thus treating the two services as independent of one another. In saying this, an IT cannot be booked on its own. It needs to be accompanied by a lesson package booking. However, the situation is different for existing students of GDS, who do not pass their initial drive test. Such students can book an IRT all on its own, (without the need to attach a lesson package), if they are confident enough to undertake a re-test without doing additional lessons.

  • If a student pays for an IT and later changes their mind, e.g. decides that they would prefer to use their own vehicle for the test, only two-thirds of the IT cost will be refunded. As such, the refund will be $60 for a cancelled IT, with one-third ($30) amounting to a cancellation fee.

  • Similarly, the refund for a cancelled IRT is $50, with one-third ($25) amounting to a cancellation fee.
