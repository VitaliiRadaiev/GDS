  • How do I book with you?

    Currently, there isn’t a payment gateway on this website, as I need to regulate the number of new students I take on.

    As such, please reach out to me either through the:

    • Contact Us section of this website
    • Phone me directly (note: if I don’t pick up, just leave a voicemail. I’ll return your call asap)
    • Send me an email (ensure to include your mobile number in the email).

    Once you’ve settled on a particular package, (be it lessons-only or lessons and test), I will then issue you an invoice that will contain payment instructions. Once payment is received, I will reach out to you once more to schedule in our first lesson.
